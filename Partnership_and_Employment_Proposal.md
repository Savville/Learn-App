# Learn Opportunities Kenya: Partnership & Employment Proposal

Welcome to **Learn Opportunities Kenya**. This document outlines how our platform operates, our revenue model, and presents two distinct pathways for you to join us: as a **Lifetime Partner** sharing in the long-term vision, or as an **Employee** helping drive our immediate growth, publicity, and content management.

---

## 1. How the Business Runs

Learn Opportunities Kenya is an innovative platform specifically tailored for university students and young professionals. Our core focus is to bridge the gap between academia and industry. 

**The Platform Mechanics:**
- **Curated Opportunities:** We host a curated selection of Call for Papers, Internships, Grants, Conferences, and Scholarships. 
- **The Escrow ATS System:** The biggest differentiator of our platform is our built-in **Escrow Applicant Tracking System (ATS)**. Employers and researchers post "micro-gigs" or projects and deposit the funds upfront into our Escrow system. Students apply via our Custom Form Builder, and once the work is approved, they get paid natively. This guarantees security for both the student (payment is assured) and the employer (work is delivered).

## 2. How We Make Money (Revenue Model)

Our monetization strategy is designed to be highly scalable:

1. **Escrow Transaction Fees:** As we facilitate paid micro-gigs and projects, the platform deducts an automated transaction fee from the Escrow payout. The more jobs deposited, the higher our transaction revenue.
2. **Featured Opportunities:** Universities, NGOs, and corporations can pay to have their opportunities or programs "Featured" at the top of our listings or in our newsletters.
3. **Premium Partnerships & Ads:** Collaborations with educational brands, tech companies, and universities who want to target an engaged, verified student demographic in Kenya.

## 3. Two Paths to Join Us

We are offering you the flexibility to choose the role that best aligns with your ambitions.

### Option A: Lifetime Partner
If you choose to join as a Partner, you will become a co-owner of the business. 

- **Your Role:** You will be directly involved in high-level strategic decisions, business development, scaling the platform to other universities, and building B2B relationships.
- **Profitability:** As a partner, your returns are tied to the platform's overall profitability (through equity or profit-sharing). As we scale our Escrow transactions and onboard hundreds of paying employers, the recurring revenue becomes substantial. This is a high-reward path for someone willing to invest their time and strategic expertise into building the company.

### Option B: Employee (Growth Lead & Content Manager)
If you prefer a structured, milestone-based compensation model, you can join as an employee taking on the specific micro-gig we currently have open. 

- **Role:** Growth Lead & Content Manager (KU Campus Ambassador)
- **Compensation:** **10,000 KES** (Stipend), fully protected by our M-PESA Escrow service. 
- **Timeline:** There is no strict deadline. You are paid as soon as you hit each specific task/KPI.

**Your Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):**

1. **Ground Community Building (KES 2,500):**
   - Build and independently manage an official Opportunities Kenya WhatsApp Community to 500 verified KU student members.

2. **Employer Onboarding (KES 3,500):**
   - Onboard 10 real professionals, researchers, or SMEs to post legitimate opportunities.
   - At least 3 of these must be paid micro-internships natively protected by our Escrow system.

3. **Application Demand (KES 2,000):**
   - Ensure the platform receives an average of 30 unique, completed applications via our Custom ATS across the jobs you onboard.

4. **Content Management & LinkedIn (KES 2,000):**
   - Maintain active content flow by posting 2 to 3 high-quality academic or professional opportunities per day, split across the Opportunities Kenya website and the official LinkedIn Newsletter.

- **Profitability:** This path guarantees a fast, predictable return for your immediate effort, with the potential for ongoing gigs and continuous income as you onboard more employers.
