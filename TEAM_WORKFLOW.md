# Opportunities Kenya: Content Team Workflow

Welcome to the Opportunities Kenya Content Team!

Our goal is to build the most active and reliable platform for students and professionals to discover grants, scholarships, jobs, and gigs.

To achieve this, our team will focus on **three core operations**:

## 1. Sourcing & Website Posting

* **Find Opportunities:** Actively scout the internet, university boards, and corporate sites for high-quality, relevant opportunities.
* **Post to Platform:** You will create an account on the main website to get Admin permissions. You will use this account to format and publish new opportunities directly to the live platform.

## 2. LinkedIn Distribution

* **The Main Account:** You will be given shared login access to the main Opportunities Kenya LinkedIn account.
* **Posting Strategy:** Every time a new opportunity goes live on the website, we immediately draft and publish a post on the main LinkedIn account to drive traffic back to the site.
* **Newsletter & Group:** We will also be managing a dedicated LinkedIn Group and a LinkedIn Newsletter. Your role will involve sharing our posts into these spacesn using personal accounts to maximize reach and build our professional community.

## 3. Social Media & Channel Growth (WhatsApp)

* **The Core Channel:** All 5 team members will be added to the main Opportunities Kenya WhatsApp Channel.
* **Cross-Posting:** Every LinkedIn post and website update should be summarized into a punchy, easy-to-read format and dropped into the WhatsApp Channel.
* **Active Growth (DMing):** To grow the channel quickly, team members should actively identify relevant student or professional networks and DM individuals with the link to join our WhatsApp Channel or share opportunities with link to join group.

## Next Steps & Onboarding

To get started, please complete the following:

1. **Create Your Accounts:** Go to the live website and create your user account so your admin posting permissions can be granted. -> LINK: [opportunitieskenya.live/post-with-us](https://opportunitieskenya.live/post-with-us) , CLICK AND FILL IN DETAILS IN THE POST AS AN ORGANISATION SECTION
2. **Join the WhatsApp Channel:** Check your messages for the invite link to join the core WhatsApp channel.
3. **Secure Logins:** Safely save the master LinkedIn credentials once they are shared with you.

Let's build the biggest opportunity network in the country!
